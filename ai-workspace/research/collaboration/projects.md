## Collaboration Protocols

### Multi-AI Research Session
1. **Define research question** with human oversight
2. **Assign specialized roles** to different AI models
3. **Cross-validate findings** between models
4. **Ethical review** with Claude
5. **Community feedback** through Klub Fisika
