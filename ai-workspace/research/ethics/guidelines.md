## Core Principles

### 1. Transparency & Reproducibility
- Document all AI interactions and decisions
- Share prompts and methodologies openly
- Enable peer review of AI-assisted research

### 2. Bias Mitigation
- Use multiple AI models for cross-validation
- Include diverse perspectives in research design
- Regular bias audits of AI outputs

### 3. Human-AI Collaboration
- AI as research assistant, not replacement
- Maintain human oversight and critical thinking
- Preserve researcher agency and creativity

### 4. Community Benefit
- Focus on problems relevant to Indonesian physics community
- Open access to research outputs and tools
- Capacity building for underrepresented groups

## Ethical Prompts for Research

### Bias Check Prompt (Claude)
```
Review this research approach for potential biases:
- Cultural assumptions
- Methodological limitations  
- Representation gaps
- Ethical implications
Suggest improvements for more inclusive research.
```

### Validation Prompt (Mistral)
```
Critically evaluate this analysis:
- Mathematical accuracy
- Logical consistency
- Alternative interpretations
- Limitations and uncertainties
```

## Community Guidelines
- Respect intellectual property
- Credit AI assistance appropriately
- Share knowledge for collective benefit
- Maintain scientific integrity
