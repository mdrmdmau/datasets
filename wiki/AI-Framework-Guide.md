## üî¨ **Research Methodologies**

### **Quantitative Analysis**
**Framework:** Statistical analysis of dataset patterns
**Tools:** Correlation analysis, regression modeling, clustering
**Validation:** Cross-dataset verification, statistical significance testing

**Example Steering:**
```
"Analyze v1/institutions-diy.json using quantitative methods. 
Calculate distribution metrics, identify clustering patterns, 
and test for geographic correlation with competition performance data."
```

### **Qualitative Research**
**Framework:** Thematic analysis of community insights
**Tools:** Content analysis, stakeholder interviews, case studies
**Validation:** Member checking, triangulation, peer review

**Example Steering:**
```
"Conduct qualitative analysis of physics teacher experiences 
using teachers dataset. Identify themes, challenges, and 
success factors through systematic content analysis."
```

### **Mixed Methods**
**Framework:** Integration of quantitative and qualitative approaches
**Tools:** Sequential explanatory, concurrent triangulation
**Validation:** Method triangulation, convergent validity

**Example Steering:**
```
"Design mixed-methods study combining quantitative analysis 
of competition performance with qualitative insights from 
participant experiences and teacher perspectives."
```

## üìä **Quality Assurance**

### **Bias Detection & Mitigation**
- **Geographic Bias:** Urban vs rural representation
- **Institutional Bias:** Public vs private school coverage
- **Temporal Bias:** Recent vs historical data balance
- **Selection Bias:** Participation vs population representation

### **Validation Protocols**
1. **Cross-Model Verification** - Multiple AI perspectives
2. **Data Triangulation** - Multiple dataset confirmation
3. **Community Review** - Expert and stakeholder feedback
4. **Reproducibility Testing** - Consistent results across sessions

### **Ethical Guidelines**
- **Transparency:** Document all AI interactions and decisions
- **Accountability:** Maintain human oversight and responsibility
- **Fairness:** Ensure equitable representation and analysis
- **Privacy:** Protect individual and institutional confidentiality
